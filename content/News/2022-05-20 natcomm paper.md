#

**New paper in Nature Communications**: Our [paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-022-30390-9), "Codon-specific Ramachandran plots show amino acid backbone conformation depends on identity of the translated codon" has been published in Nature Communications.

In this work we applied powerful statistical methods to uncover novel associations between synonymous genetic coding and protein structure.
